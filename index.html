Who/What are the Cutie Mark Crusaders?

The Cutie Mark Crusaders (or CMC for short) are a group of fillies who are intent on attaining their special talent, represented by the Cutie Mark that appears on their flanks. The Ponyville branch currently consists of four members: Scootaloo the Pegasus; Applebloom the Earth Pony; Sweetie Belle the Unicorn; and honorary member Discord the Draconequus. The Manehattan branch currently only has one known member: Babs Seed the Earth Pony.

So... wait, what’s the point of this site?

Honestly it’s just to make this portion of internet more exciting than just a parked page (and because cutiemarkcrusaders.club seemed like a clever domain). My main plan with this site is to create a site made from the collaborative ideas of the Bronies Australia internet forum. Almost anything can go, and you can join in too by going to the Bronies Australia thread and leaving your own suggestion!

“Almost” anything can go?

Basically, I want this to be marginally acceptable in case any of my relatives happen to stumble across this site, so nothing NSFW and no Comic Sans (bar Discord’s page).
